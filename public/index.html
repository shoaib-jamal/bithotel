Welcome to the BitHotel

A pixel based 2d multiplayer social environment for people all over the world to meet and interact with each other. Here, you can chat with others, explore different rooms, hangout with pet harvey in the backyard, sing with the alphabets in the rhyme room, or enjoy the comfort in VIP room.

This platform is built open source by Shoaib Jamal, and based on LIKELIKE Online.

Feel free to apply for adminship on this server and get access to several commands such as sending special messages, kicking users, or banning IPs
